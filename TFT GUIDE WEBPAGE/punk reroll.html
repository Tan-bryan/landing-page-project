This comp uses 3 star Jinx as your reroll Punk carry and Pantheon as your 3 star Punk tank!

WHEN TO MAKE

- If you see Jinx Headliner - If you see a Guardian Headliner (ideally Pantheon)

HOW TO PLAY

- Play around Jinx - Make sure to ALWAYS use the 1 gold Punk shop refresh! DON'T level up early!

- At 3-1 roll down to 30 gold and buy every Jinx/Vi copy Level up to 6 AFTER hitting Jinx 3 star

If you see a Guardian headliner, rush for level 8 after hitting Jinx and Pantheon 3 stars so that you can play 6 Guardian at level 8.
